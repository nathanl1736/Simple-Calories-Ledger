Calories Banking

Calories banking treats your calorie goal as a weekly budget, not a perfect daily pass/fail.

  • Under your goal? You bank the unused calories.
  • Over your goal? You spend some of the bank.
  • Only completed days count. Press Complete the day when your food log is final.
  • Reopening a day updates the bank. This keeps the weekly numbers honest if you change an older day.
Example: 1,800 kCal/day = 12,600 kCal/week. If Monday is 1,600, you bank +200. If Tuesday is 2,000, you use -200.

The graph shows each day's impact. Bars above the middle line are banked calories. Bars below the line are calories used from the bank.

Weekly Adherence

Weekly adherence shows how many completed days in the selected week were on track.

  • Cutting mode: a successful day is at or under your calorie budget.
  • Maintain or Bulking mode: a successful day is no more than 100 kCal below target. Going over target still counts as on track for these modes.
  • Only completed days count. Open days are shown, but are not included in the score.
Tip: Use the week arrows to review past weeks, or tap the Stats tab again to return all Stats cards to this week.
